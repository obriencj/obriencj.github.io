About Me

My name is Christopher O’Brien, but I typically go by the monicker ‘siege.’ The story behind this is significantly less interesting than you might imagine. When I was young, my siblings and relatives called me by my initials, ‘CJ.” The nickname ‘siege’ is simply a verbal permutation of that which emerged over time and happened to stick.

Now that we have that exciting tidbit out of the way…

I am the proud father of a beautiful little girl named Zoe. She loves riding her bike and playing Minecraft, and she is the light of my life.

I work at ridiculous projects in the wee hours of the night. Frequently things that nobody would (nor should) actually use. However, I do not view this time as wasted. These mad hacks are a creative exercise, and I find that my mad re-inventions can provide excellent insight into otherwise inobvious situations.

I work with Red Hat in the role of a Release Engineer, wherein I get to apply the saner aspects of my programming skills to the domain of building and releasing software.

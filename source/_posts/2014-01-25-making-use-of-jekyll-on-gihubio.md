I've finally got a setup beyond a quick [multimarkdown]-generated
page. I'm considering the posibilities. Is this more convenient or
better than the tumblr I'd been maintaining? I'm not sure. I know
these pages get to take advantage of the CDN. That's a bonus. I also
know that I have direct access to the full history and content of all
of my posts immediately -- also a bonus.

But I am not very happy with the theming right now. I've had to make a
number of changes to what should have been a functioning theme (but
which wasn't). Do I want to get invested in layout design as part of
this? Not really, no. But it may be worth the effort.

Things to consider on a lazy and cold Sunday.
